Warm choco cupcakes

Description

Experience pure comfort in every bite with these warm chocolate cupcakes. Moist and rich, they're perfect for cozy evenings or any celebration. Simply dusted with sugar, they're an irresistible treat that will satisfy your chocolate cravings and warm your soul.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 60g sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 50g dark chocolate (64% cocoa), cut into pieces
  • 50g butter
  • white flour
  • some icing sugar

Steps

  1. Mix the egg, egg yolk, sugar and salt in a bowl with a hand mixer until the mixture turns lighter in colour.
  2. Melt the chocolate and butter in a thin-sided bowl over a gently boiling bain-marie stirring until smooth.
  3. Add to the egg mixture.
  4. Fold the flour into the mix and pour into the prepared cups.
  5. Place the cups on an oven tray and bake for approx. 18 mins. in the centre of an oven preheated to 180Â°C.
  6. Remove, dust with icing sugar, and serve immediately.

Good to know

Prepare the chocolate cup cakes approx. half a day in advance, ready for baking, cover and keep in the fridge. The baking time will then be approx. 7 minutes longer.
