Zurich pita pockets

Description

Discover a taste sensation with this Pita Pocket recipe! Soft pitas embrace a variety of savory fillings, creating a satisfying meal or snack bursting with Mediterranean flavors.

Quick to make and delightful to devour, Pita Pockets are perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 2 tsb clarified butter
  • 200 g vegetarian strips
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 100g mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 ½ tbsp white flour
  • ½ dl white wine
  • 2 dl cream
  • 6 mini pitas

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tbsp of clarified butter in a non-stick frying pan.
  2. Add the thin slices of meat and fry, turning occasionally, for approx. 4 mins., then remove and set aside.
  3. Reduce the heat, add the white wine and allow to reduce to half the amount before adding the cream.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add the slices of veggie strips or meat and heat gently.
  6. Slice the pitta breads, heat in the microwave (20 seconds at 600W) and fill them.
